ABOUT US



AAYUSHI GUPTA

CEO of Shopoholics
Shopoholics, Inc. is an Indian multinational conglomerate technology company based in Seattle, with 750 employees. It focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It is considered one of the Big Four tech companies, along with Google, Apple, and Microsoft.It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world."

Shopoholics is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale. It is the world's largest online marketplace, AI assistant provider, and cloud computing platform as measured by revenue and market capitalization. Shopoholics is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world. It is the second largest private employer in the India and one of the world's most valuable companies.

Shopoholics was founded by Aayushi Gupta. The company initially started as an online marketplace for books but later expanded to sell electronics, software, video games, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and jewelry.capitalization.
